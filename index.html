Activities & Leadership

Founder & President

SCUProductSpace Product Management Club

scuproduct.org

I found myself navigating a field that was exciting, but difficult to break into. I realized that while interest in PM was growing on campus, resources and guidance were limited. I constantly ran into the same challenges: No clear roadmap for how to prepare, little visibility into real-world PM expectations, and few chances to connect with mentors or recruiters. I created ProductSpace to change that, a place where any student, from any background, could explore PM through community, events, and hands-on experiences.

Student Ambassador

When I was touring universities as a prospective student, I remember how disorienting it felt to walk through campus after campus without seeing many people who looked like me. Since then, I've led over 100 tours and panels, adapting my message to connect with diverse audiences. I've trained 30 new ambassadors and was featured in university campaigns viewed by 1,000+ students, always aiming to create a more inclusive and welcoming experience.
